About Us

SreeKaram Enterprises is an ISO 22000:2018 certified manufacturer, supplier and exporter of wide range of products which include Turmeric powder, Red Chilli powder, Coriander powder, Garam Masala, Sambar Powder, Grains & Rices, Pluses and Dry Fruits. The products available are processed and packed with utmost precision, all our spices and food stuffs are superior in quality and retain their properties of flavoring for long period of time. We follow industry guidelines and ensure that finest grade of products are delivered to consumers

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be the preferred supplier of grounded spices and pulses to our discerning clients by maintaining the highest standards of quality and creating products that satisfy our customers.

Our Mission

To serve our customers, partners and associates through innovative, reliable, value for money and efficient equipment trading and associated services; rendered with speed, transparency, reliability with integrity, safety and efficiency.
To create value and meet all expectations of the stakeholders through unique combination of experience, talent, knowledge, skills and technology.
